About Us

Welcome to Lucky Paw!

We are a non-profit animal shelter dedicated to providing a safe and loving haven for pets in need. Founded in 2005 by a group of passionate animal lovers, Lucky Paw was born out of a desire to make a difference in the lives of companion animals. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome companion animals, giving them a second chance at a happy and healthy life. From playful kittens to gentle senior dogs, our furry friends are waiting for their forever homes.

Volunteeering

We couldn't do it without the help of our amazing community! If you're passionate about making a difference in the lives of animals, we invite you to join us as a volunteer. Whether you're able to help with daily tasks like cleaning and feeding, or assist with special projects like fundraising or event planning, we welcome your energy and enthusiasm.

Donations

We rely on donations from generous supporters like you to continue our work. Whether it's a one-time gift or a recurring donation, every contribution helps us provide vital care and services to the animals in our care. Please consider supporting our mission by donating today!

Your best friend is waiting for you!

Ready to get involved? Fill out our Get in Touch form to schedule a visit to meet our adorable pets, to help as a volunteer, and adopt your new furry friend! Our friendly team will be happy to answer any questions you may have. Whether you're looking to spend time with our furry friends or become a part of our amazing community of animal lovers, we can't wait to hear from you!
